# Permguard Maintainers

This document defines the scope and limits of the *Maintainer* role for the
Permguard repositories.

Maintainer status concerns **repository operations only** and does **not**
create, transfer, modify, or imply:

- ownership of Permguard or its intellectual property,
- legal, professional, or operational responsibility for Permguard,
- authority to bind or create obligations for Nitro Agility S.r.l.

---

## Responsibilities

Maintainers MAY:

- manage repository operations,
- review contributions,
- merge changes,
- coordinate issues and documentation.

Maintainers MAY NOT:

- alter ownership or attribution,
- create obligations, warranties, or liabilities regarding Permguard,
- grant or transfer maintainer status.

---

## Role Assignment and Changes

Maintainer roles:

- are assigned and modified solely by Nitro Agility S.r.l.,
- cannot be self-assigned,
- cannot be transferred,
- cannot be granted or altered through pull requests.

Any attempt to imply otherwise through documentation, repository metadata,
or project process is invalid.
